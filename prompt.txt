OBSERVED PROBLEMS IN PRODUCTION:

When a user asks "what is your office address" or "where are you located", the bot responds with vague answers like "we're based in Lagos, Nigeria" instead of the actual street address. The full address exists in the codebase in constants.js inside a COMPANY object. The bot is not using it — it appears to be guessing from general knowledge instead.

PROBLEM 3 — PROMPT DRIFT UNDER PRODUCTION LOAD
The system has been patched multiple times via AI prompt adjustments to fix conversation behavior. Each patch fixes one thing but breaks or weakens something else. The AI is currently acting as both the conversationalist and the control plane — it decides when to push for payment, what company info to give, and how to handle trust objections. There are no code-level guardrails catching bad AI decisions. This is not sustainable as conversation volume grows.
